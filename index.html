Target zone is Hard or Failed. Easy and Good mean the weight needs to go up.

Easy — not working hard enough. Weight jumps immediately.
Good — still too comfortable. Reps jump to top of range, then weight goes up.
Hard — target zone. Reps nudge up each session. After 2 Hard sessions = weight jumps.
Failed — ideal stimulus. Weight holds, reps build. After 3 consecutive fails = slight deload (grinding ≠ growing).
